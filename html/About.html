About Us





Snazzles in an online clothing store that specializes in aiding and promoting local clothing brands and selling their products online to allow them to reach a wider audience of customers. We focus on making sure the creators we’re partnered with are satisfied with our services and prices, with reliable delivery of the products to our customers. We ship worldwide to all countries. Our hardworking staff is readily available should you need support as a customer or as a brand owner looking to partner with us in order to expand your clientele.



Our Founder and Ceo, Nole Sobez (pictured on the left) began this company as a teenager making a website with the intent of helping his mother’s homemade clothing brand reach a wider audience. Following this success, he has since been approached by numerous creators who are looking to have their products reach a wider audience as well. Snazzles is based in Mississauga, Ontario and ships all across Canada.



Growing up, Nole Sobez was always the weird kid. His reputation among his peers only got worse the bleak morning of December 16th 1985. From extreme negligence, Sobez had accidentally left his home without pants on. After this incident, Sobez made a promise to himself to never stop trying his best. He shortly began learning how to code HTML and CSS. After then, he began learning about the backend side of web programming. Soon enough, he became an expert and made his very own website, snazzles.com.
